Main Project

Temperature Blanket

Current Project: 2024 Temperature Blanket

Every day in 2024 I keep track of the high and the low temperature on a spreadsheet, the center of the granny square is the low with the outer rim being the high

Finally, the outermost layer is the color assigned to the month (all the months are based of their birth stones!)

One granny square takes me about 15 minutes

It takes about 10 minutes to stitch a row together

The finished product will have 378 squares (12 months and 366 days)

Estimated amount of time spent on squares alone: 5,670 minutes/94.5 hours/3.94 days
